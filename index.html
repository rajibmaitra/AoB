Make AOB your Home

AOB is a collaborative endeavor driven by the desire to create a vibrant Bay Area Bengali community. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey of connection, celebration, and empowerment. Your participation adds resonance to our collective voice...



Vision & Mission

One Platform for all Bengalis

Vision : AoB is dedicated to nurturing and advancing BengaliCulture, preserving our rich Heritage, and promoting a spirit of Innovation.


Mission: AoB aspires to strengthen the Bengali community, cultivate belonging, and catalyze inclusion through providing a common platform.

Core Values

Cohesive Community

Harmony, Inclusion, Transparency, Respect & Accountability


Open to Individuals, Families, Groups and Associations passionate about Bengali culture, literature arts

How We Operate

Membership: AOB is open to all individuals who identify with the Bay Area Bengali community and support our goals. Members will have the opportunity to participate in events, shape the organization's direction, and enjoy exclusive benefits.

Governance and executive functions: AOB will be governed by an elected board of directors and run by functional committees. These groups will be responsible for decision-making, event planning, financial management, and ensuring the organization aligns with its mission.

FAQ

Our Initial setup

To establish a strong foundation, the founding members will appoint the first set of committee leaders. These committees will be instrumental in developing core programs and activities.

How can you get involved

Volunteer:

Spread the Word:
Help us raise awareness about AOB within the Bay Area Bengali community. Let's Build a Stronger Community Together.
