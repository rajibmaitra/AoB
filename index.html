Who we are

AoB is a collaborative endeavor driven by the desire to create an inclusive and vibrant Bengali community. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey of connection, celebration, and empowerment. Your participation adds resonance to our collective voice...



Vision & Mission

One Platform for all Bengalis


Vision : AoB is dedicated to nurturing and advancing BengaliCulture, preserving our rich Heritage, and promoting a spirit of Innovation.

Mission: AoB aspires to strengthen the Bengali community, cultivate belonging, and catalyze inclusion through providing a common platform.

Core Values

Cohesive Community, Harmony, Inclusion, Transparency, Respect & Accountability

Open to Individuals, Families, Groups and Associations passionate about Bengali culture, literature arts

How We Operate


Membership:
AOB is open to all individuals who identify with the Bay Area Bengali community and support our goals. Members will have the opportunity to participate in events, shape the organization's direction, and enjoy exclusive benefits.

Governance and executive functions:
AOB will be governed by an elected board of directors and run by functional committees. These groups will be responsible for decision-making, event planning, financial management, and ensuring the organization aligns with its mission.

FAQ

Our Initial setup

To establish a strong foundation, the founding members will appoint the first set of committee leaders. These committees will be instrumental in developing core programs and activities.


How can you get involved

Collaboration:

Spread the Word:

The Coffee House

A Place for Bengalis, by Bengalis

Imagine a place where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the warmth of nostalgia, where conversations flow as freely as ideas, and where the spirit of the Bengali community thrives. The Bengali Coffee House is more than just a café—it's a cultural hub, a meeting ground, and a home away from home.


How You Can Be a Part of This Movement

💡 Join the Team:
Help us shape the vision and build the foundation of the Bengali Coffee House. Your expertise, creativity, and enthusiasm can bring this dream to life.

💰 Fundraising:
Every great idea needs support. Be a part of something bigger—contribute and help us turn this vision into reality.

📊 Financial Model:
We're open to ideas! How can we create a self-sustaining coffee house in the Bay Area? Share your insights and let's make it happen together.

🤝 Platform Partnership:
This is a collaborative space. AoB provides the platform—you bring the passion. Let's build something that represents our community's spirit and creativity.

📣 Spread the Word:
This is OUR dream. Can't we make it happen—TOGETHER?



Our Roadmap: A Community-Driven Bengali Coffee House

🚀 Phase 1: Bringing People Together
Host year-round Bengali cultural events in the Bay Area—celebrating our heritage, art, and traditions.

🎭 Phase 2: Scaling Up
Expand into a large-scale annual Bengali festival, a grand celebration of food, literature, music, and sports.

🏡 Phase 3: The Coffee House Becomes Reality
Establish a physical Bengali Center—a permanent space for cultural activities, intellectual discussions, sports, and community gatherings.

This is more than a Coffee House—it's a legacy in the making.
